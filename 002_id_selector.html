Importance of Personal Hygiene

Washing your hands regularly prevents the spread of germs.

Use soap and water or a hand sanitizer with alcohol.

Wash hands before eating,and after using the restroom.

Brush your teeth twice daily to avoid cavities.

Use dental floss to clean between teeth.

Regular dental checkups helps to maintain oral healt.

Bathing daily remove dirts and sweats from your skin.

Use deodorant to reduce body odor.

Wearing clean cloths is also path of body hygiene.

Trim your nails regularly to prevents dirts buildup.

Avoid biting your nails as it can transfer germs.

Clean under your nails with a brush or file.

Wash your hair regularly with good hair products.

Keep your hair neat and avoid sharing comb.

Use good hair conditioner to keep hair soft and managable.
